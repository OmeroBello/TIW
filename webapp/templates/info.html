Information page regarding EE :)

Are you wishing to do an Erasmus Experience but you don't know where to search for courses?

Are you tired of surfing on Universities' websites looking for courses where findind the secret of happines seems to be easier?

Are you an University and do you wish to empower the connections between European youths in the near future?

EE has the answer for all of these!

EE is a application website co-founded by the Erasmus+ Programme of the European Union which wants to follow and help Erasmus students by giving them an easy and cler tool to search for compatible courses with their academic career.

Here, you'll be able to search for courses by Universities and to read information about them!

Other than that, you'll be able also to create a simplified Learning Agreement where to check your convertions and ECTS number!

Are you an University? You'll be able to load in the application's database your courses so they'll show up to students searching for them!
